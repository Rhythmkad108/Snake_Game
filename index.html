🐍 Snake Fun

Snake Fun Facts

  • Some sea snakes can breathe partially through their skin, allowing them to stay underwater longer.
  • Snakes smell with their tongue and can even tell which direction a smell is coming from.
  • They are “solar-powered” and rely fully on external heat or light sources.🌞
  • Snakes don't have eyelids.
  • The fastest snake can travel up to 12 miles per hour.
  • The longest snake is the reticulated python (6 meters long).

Rules

  • Snake is hungry! Help him get the food.🍎
  • Don't let the snake touch the boundary.
  • Don't let the snake eat itself. 🐍
